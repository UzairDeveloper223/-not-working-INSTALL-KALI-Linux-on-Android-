Chairman's Message

Prof. Nazar Hussain Ch.

It has been a great honour for me to become head of an institution that follows a completely transparent and computerized examination system by the grace of Allah Almighty. All the departments are well aware of their duties. The dedication and determination of both technical and non-technical staff is quite impressive. The knowledge-based understanding of the employees regarding rules and regulations of examination is excellent. My main objective is to preserve and prosper the on going system of the organization in such a way that it becomes more strengthened and modernized. While on the other side I have plans to facilitate people as much as possible keeping the sanctity of the rules making more positive changes in the system. I am also very keen to improve the standard of education and provide comfortable environment for everyone who likes to get the light of education. I am sure that the new generation is quite energetic and enthusiastic and if given positive directions they can make us feel proud. I feel that the talented people must be encouraged and appreciated and talent must get its reward. I have also invited the experienced technical professionals of my organization to come and share their ideas to find new paradigms for the betterment of education and I am also very willing to convert any positive idea into reality by the grace of Allah Almighty.
